About us


This tool is developed in view of both, the recruiter as well as the intern/student/employee.
Generally what happens is that after the interview is completed the candidate is only informed whether he/she is selected or rejected. But, the cadidate has no idea about the reason behind the selection or rejection (whatever the case may be) nor the recruiters have that much of time to explain them the respective reasons.

So, we thought of developing a tool which will, without taking much time of the recruiter, help them grade the cadidate on their performance in interview.
